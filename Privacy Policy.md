# Privacy Policy for `ColourPicture`
#### Last Updated: March 2026

## At `ColourPicture`, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This document outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it.

### 1. Image Processing (Client-Side)
Our Image Color Picker tool processes all images locally in your browser.

We do not upload, store, or share your images on any server.

Once you close the browser tab, the image data is cleared from your device's temporary memory (RAM).

### 2. Local Storage
We use "Local Storage" to remember your theme preference (Light Mode or Dark Mode). This data remains on your device and is not accessible by us.

### 3. Log Files and Analytics
Like most websites, we may use standard log files or basic analytics (like Google Analytics) to see how many people visit the site. This includes:

Internet Protocol (IP) addresses.

Browser type and version.

Time and date of visit.

Pages visited.

This data is used solely for analyzing trends and improving the website.

### 4. Cookies and Web Beacons
If we display advertisements (such as Google AdSense), third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your Google Ad Settings.

### 5. Contact Information
If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us via our Feedback Form or Discord.
